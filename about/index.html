About us

We think like operators, not spectators.

Naughty Bean Consulting exists because the best advice usually comes from people who have to live with the consequences of execution.

Our consulting work sits at the intersection of coffee shop hospitality and software engineering. That mix gives us a grounded view of customer experience, operational detail, and the digital systems that support both.

What shapes us

Naughty Bean Coffee brings the physical side: service flow, product quality, brand atmosphere, and the realities of running a customer-facing space.

ClickCollectCoffee brings the software side: ecommerce thinking, order-ahead experiences, digital trust, and operational tooling.

How we work

We prefer clear recommendations, direct communication, and deliverables that can actually be used. We are comfortable advising, designing, and building, depending on what the project needs.

That means we can stay strategic without drifting into vague language or sterile corporate filler.

Hospitality lens

We care about queue friction, product presentation, customer mood, repeat habits, and what a brand feels like in the room.

Engineering lens

We care about maintainability, sensible architecture, shipping with intent, and avoiding overbuilt systems that become expensive ornaments.
